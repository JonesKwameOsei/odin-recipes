Buffalo Chicken Wing Sauce

Home

Description

Hers's the delicious Buffalo-flavoured chicken wing sauce. It's quick, simple and easy to make sauce that's booming with flavour. And all it takes are a few minutes on the stove.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot)
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter
  • 11/2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a pot over medium heat. Bring to a simmer while stiring with a whisk.
  3. As soon as the liquid begins to bubble on the side of the pot, remove from heat, stir with the whisk, and set aside for use.
  4. Enjoy!
